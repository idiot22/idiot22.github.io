    # 句子

    1. the last people: 最后的人

    They were the last people you'd expect to be involved in anything strange or mysterious

    1. hold with 赞同，相信

    nonsense 荒谬的念头，事情

    they just didn't hold with such nonsense.

    1. the director of a firm 公司的主管

    drill钻头

    Mr. Dursley was the director of a firm called Grunnings, which made drills

    1. beefy: 结实的 hardly any: 几乎没有

    He was a big, beefy man with hardly any neck

    1. blonde: 白皙的金发碧眼的 the amount of 不可数: ...的长度

    Mrs. Dursley was thin and blonde and had nearly twice the usual amount of neck

    good-for-nothing: 一无是处 as A as B 像B一样A as tall as you are 越。。越好: as tall as possible

    her sister and her good-for-nothing husband were as unDursleyish as it was possible to be

    Dursley gossiped away happily as she wrestled a screaming Dudley into hishigh chair

    1. have a tantrum 发脾气 cereal 麦片，谷物

    Dudleywas now having a tantrum and throwing his cereal at the walls.

    1. the first sign of ...的迹象

    he noticed the first sign of somethingpeculiar

    1. jerk 猛拉 jerk ... around猛然回头

    he jerked his head around to look again

    1. getup 装束，穿戴

    the getups you saw on young people

    1. a trick of ...的技巧 a trick of the light 幻觉

    It must have been a trick of the light

    1. drum：敲打 steering wheel 方向盘

    He drummed his fingers on the steering wheel

    1. be enraged to 被。。激怒

    Mr. Dursley was enraged to see that a couple of them weren't young at all

    14.struck,strick 罢工，撞击

    silly stunt愚蠢的特技

    it struck Mr. Dursley that this was probably some silly stunt

    1. with sbs back to sth: 背靠着

    Mr. Dursley always sat with his back to the window

    1. gaze open-mouthed 目瞪口呆

    they pointed and gazed open-mouthed as owl even at nighttime
